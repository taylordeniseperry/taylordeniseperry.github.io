beauTV Live! Instructions

Broadcaster Instuctions

  • Software Setup
    • Download a free video encoder (OBS) for your operating system (Windows, Mac, Linux) https://obsproject.com/download
    • Install OBS by doubling clicking the downloaded file
    • Open the OBS program
  • Camera Setup
    • Connect your digital camera/web cam to your computer if not done already.
    • Within the OBS program, click the + icon under the Sources
    • Select Video Capture Device > Create New > OK
    • Under Device: Select you digital camera, Ensure the window is displaying an image from the camera.
    • Under Preset, select 1280x720 or the highest resolution available (top of the list). Click OK
    • Back in OBS, click the Picture feed from the Camera in the Preview window so it is highlighted in red.
    • Hit CMD + C to center the preview, then CMD + F to fit it to the screen. OR Right click on the preview and select transform > center in frame followed by fit to screen
  • Live Stream Setup
    • Click SETTINGS (Bottom left corner)
      • Click Output
        • Output Mode Select Advanced
        • Under Bitrate enter 700
        • In Keyframe Interval enter 2
      • Click Video
        • In Output (Scaled) Resolution field enter (type-in or copy-paste) 854x480
      • Click Stream
        • Under Stream Type, Select Custom Streaming Server
        • In the URL field, copy and paste the StreamURL emailed to you from PlumPerfect
        • In the Stream Key field, copy and paste the StreamKEY emailed to you from PlumPerfect
      • Click OK to save settings
  • Click "Start Streaming" to begin your tutorial session. You can watch the video and see comments on youtube at the Event URL specified in your Confirmation Email

Event Coordinator Instructions

  • Login to YouTube
  • Click the round profile icon in top right corner, select creator studio
  • Select LIVE STREMING, Third option from the top in the Left Column under Creator Studio
    • Click Events under live streaming
    • Select the Event that you will be starting from the list
