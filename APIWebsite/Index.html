MAKEUP POLICE API + WIDGET


Seeking a new way to engage your readers while optimizing your affiliate links? The Makeup Police widget is a simple way to do both.


Here's How It Works


  • User uploads a selfie to the widget.
  • They choose a makeup product to see if it is or is not a match for them
  • Our technology analyzes the user’s color signature to determine if it is a match.
  • If the product is a match, the product will pop up and link to your affiliate link.

Benefits


  • A unique way to optimize your affiliate links
  • A new way to engage with your readers
  • A higher likelihood for sales when a user clicks on your affiliate links

Features


  • Upload a set of NUMBER makeup products of your choice
  • Insert your affiliate links to increase clicks--when users engage it’ll lead to more clicks and sales for you!
